Vision

To be a Christ centered church and spread the love of God through word and deed in the light of Lutheran Doctrines.

Mission

To serve as a community of worship and fellowship, and reach people for Christ, to integrate them into the church; to nurture them toward Christian maturity. . We reach people with the love of Christ, providing for their needs, praying with them, and encouraging them.
